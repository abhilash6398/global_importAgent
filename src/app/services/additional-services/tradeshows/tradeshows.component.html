Why You Need a US Import Agent for Tradeshows

Before exporting your product to the US, its always best to test the water for demand and find some customers who want to buy or distribute your product. Trade-shows play a vital part in introducing Food & Beverage products to the US market and gauging popularity. At US Import Agent, we understand this. That is why we offer specifically tailored services for Foreign Food Facilities planning to attend Trade-shows within the USA.


Contrary to popular opinion, Food & Beverages brought into the US for trade show purposes are NOT exempt from FDA regulations because it is intended for distribution to the public. FDA Compliance can be complicated, and that is why you need expert advice.

If you bring food into the United States for the purposes of a trade show without the proper FSVP (Foreign Supplier Verification Program activities) completed, then you are breaking US Law your future imports could be banned at the port of entry into the US. This contradicts the reason for doing a trade show and jeopardises your investment.

The FDA are well known for attending the trade shows and going around exhibitors and asking them for their FSVP credentials and paperwork for bringing food into the country, Don't jeopardise your future, let us help.
