Our Foreign Food Facility Exporter Services

The introduction of the Food Safety Modernization Act (FSMA) has turned food safety compliance requirements on its head for exporters of food to the U.S.

As stated by the FDA :


"All FDA-regulated products imported into the United States are required to meet the same laws and regulations as domestic goods. Imported foods must be pure, wholesome, safe to eat and produced under sanitary conditions and must contain informative and truthful labelling in English."

As a result, all foreign food facilities are now required to re-register with the FDA every two years and produce food in a manner which meets the standards of the new FSMA Preventive Controls for Human and Animal Food regulations and new FSMA Produce Safety Rule.
