Why You Need an FSVP Import Agent

If you are a food producer, processor or packer who exports food products to the USA and you do not have a US based customer or 'consignee' who is willing to take legal responsibility for FSVP (FDA's Foreign Supplier verification Program Final Rule) then you need the services of an FSVP Import Agent.

An FSVP Agent is required because there is a legal requirement to have a US based entity who will assess the food safety compliance of foreign suppliers and the foods they export to the United States.

The purpose of this rule is to ensure that foods imported into the USA meet the same legal food safety standards that US food producers and processors are expected to comply with.

In this situation, it is likely to be more cost effective to use the services of US Import Agent than it is to establish, and staff, an office in the USA to take care of this legal requirement.
