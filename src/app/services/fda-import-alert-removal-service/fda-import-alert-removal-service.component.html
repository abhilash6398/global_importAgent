If you are on the Red List of an FDA Import Alert then your food or dietary supplement product will be detained by FDA.

The FDA can, and regularly do, add companies and products to the Red List of Import Alerts for violations of Food Drug and Cosmetics, FSMA and FSVP rules. These are regularly used by FDA, and Customs Officials, to identify food products , which have been banned from entering the US for food safety law and compliance violations.
